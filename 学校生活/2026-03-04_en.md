## Phonics

**Red Group** focused on learning the letter sounds o and c. The children practised recognising and pronouncing these sounds before completing reinforcement activities to strengthen understanding. The session concluded with writing exercises, followed by a class-wide blending practice using both new and previously learned letters to support reading development.

**Green Group** continued exploring the Set 2 sound "oo," using the rhyme "oo, look at a book." They practised reading book, look, took, shook, and cook, identifying and blending the shorter "oo" sound accurately. The group reviewed previously taught sounds and green words, read new "alien" nonsense words linked to "oo," and revisited red sight words (I, the, your, he, of, no, put, said, my). They completed their first partner reading of the phonics book My Dog Ned, followed by a full class read-through led by the teacher, and finished with handwriting practice to reinforce spelling and letter formation.

**Orange Group** focused on the Magic E pattern u-e, using the phrase "huge brute." The children confidently read words such as tune, huge, brute, use, and mule, demonstrating increasing accuracy with longer vowel sounds. They then practised writing the u-e pattern and spelling words containing this sound, showing strong effort and concentration throughout.

## Self-Exploration Time

The classroom offered the same hands-on activity stations as yesterday:

• **Animal Pattern Painting** – Children carefully observed animal fur patterns and recreated them on coloured paper.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Understanding the World: Exploring habitats, climates, and adaptation
✓ Expressive Arts & Design: Observational painting and pattern awareness
✓ Communication & Language: Explaining reasoning and observations

## Miss Naomi's Chinese Class

Today, children reviewed yesterday's measure words 棵 (for plants) and 只 (for animals) and learned a new one, 朵 (for flowers). They practised saying 一朵、两朵、三朵 to describe flowers in pictures.

Children explored colours 红色 (red) and 黄色 (yellow) and were guided to combine familiar vocabulary such as 我看见 (I see), 一朵 (one flower), 一个 (one), 一棵 (one plant), 一只 (one animal), 一-十 (numbers 1–10), 花 (flower), 草 (grass), 毛毛虫 (caterpillar) into complete sentences.

**EYFS Development Focus:**

✓ Communication & Language: Encourages children to form simple and extended sentences using new and familiar vocabulary
✓ Literacy: Supports recognition and use of measure words in context
✓ Personal, Social & Emotional Development: Promotes confidence as children share observations with peers
✓ Mathematics (Numbers & Patterns): Reinforces counting through measure words (一朵、两朵、三朵)
