# Deer Class – Daily Learning Update (Friday)

Happy Friday Deer Class families!

Today was a very special Friday at KSS as we celebrated all of our February birthdays together.

The morning was filled with excitement, laughter, and celebration as the children joined the whole school for a fun birthday event to recognise our classmates who had birthdays in February. It was a wonderful way to bring the community together and start the day with joy and positive energy.

## February Birthday Celebration

Today we celebrated all of the February birthdays at KSS. The children gathered outside with teachers and classmates to sing Happy Birthday to the birthday children. Everyone enjoyed the festive atmosphere, dancing together and sharing lots of smiles. Each child also received a small cupcake as part of the celebration, making the moment feel extra special for everyone involved.

After the singing and dancing, the celebration continued with a large open play event. Children walked with their teachers around the activity area to visit a variety of fun game stalls hosted by different teachers. They participated in different challenges, won small prizes, and even enjoyed fresh popcorn from the popcorn machine. The children loved exploring the different stations and trying each activity with their friends.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Personal, Social & Emotional Development: Celebrating classmates and sharing joyful experiences together
✓ Communication & Language: Singing, talking, and interacting with peers and teachers
✓ Physical Development: Moving, dancing, and participating in active games
✓ Characteristics of Effective Learning: Trying new activities and confidently exploring different game stations

## Self Exploration

After the birthday celebrations, the children returned to the classroom for Self Exploration time. They revisited the same activity stations from the previous days, allowing them to continue building their ideas and projects. Many children returned to favourite activities while others explored new materials, working independently or with classmates while teachers supported and guided their play.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Personal, Social & Emotional Development: Developing independence and cooperation with peers
✓ Communication & Language: Discussing ideas and explaining play choices with classmates
✓ Understanding the World: Exploring materials and using imagination during play
✓ Characteristics of Effective Learning: Engaging deeply in activities and following their own interests

## Outdoor Play

The children then spent time outdoors enjoying active play together. They ran, climbed, and played games with their friends while enjoying the fresh air. Outdoor play gives the children an important opportunity to develop their physical strength and coordination while also practising teamwork and social skills.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Physical Development: Improving coordination, balance, and overall fitness
✓ Personal, Social & Emotional Development: Learning cooperation and turn-taking
✓ Characteristics of Effective Learning: Exploring the outdoor environment with confidence

## Mathematics

During Friday Maths with Miss Naomi, the children worked carefully in their maths workbooks. The focus of today's lesson was sequencing numbers. The children practised recognising the correct order of numbers and completing activities that required them to place numbers in sequence. Everyone worked hard and showed great focus while completing their pages.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Mathematics: Understanding number order and sequencing
✓ Literacy: Recognising number symbols in written activities
✓ Communication & Language: Discussing number order and problem solving with the teacher
✓ Characteristics of Effective Learning: Concentrating and persisting when completing workbook tasks

## Lunch & Nap

After the busy morning, the children enjoyed lunch together before settling down for a restful nap. This quiet time helps the children recharge and prepare for the afternoon activities.

## Afternoon Outdoor Play

Following nap time, the children had another opportunity to play outside. They enjoyed running games, climbing activities, and spending time with their friends in the playground before returning inside for snack time.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Physical Development: Strengthening muscles through active play
✓ Personal, Social & Emotional Development: Playing cooperatively with friends
✓ Characteristics of Effective Learning: Being active and confident in physical play

## King's Time

The day finished with our special Friday session called King's Time. During this free play period, the children choose the activities they would like to explore while the teachers join them in their play. Teachers gently encourage the children to try a variety of skills such as maths, phonics, reading, and construction while still allowing them the freedom to lead their own learning.

The children enjoyed building, reading, creating, and playing imaginative games with their friends and teachers. It was a lovely way to end the week by celebrating creativity, independence, and curiosity.

**EYFS Benefits**

✓ Personal, Social & Emotional Development: Building confidence through independent choices
✓ Literacy: Practising reading and phonics during play
✓ Mathematics: Exploring counting, shapes, and numbers during construction and games
✓ Expressive Arts & Design: Encouraging creativity and imaginative play
✓ Characteristics of Effective Learning: Choosing activities, exploring ideas, and staying engaged

It has been a joyful and memorable Friday in Deer Class. The children loved celebrating their classmates' birthdays and taking part in all of the fun activities throughout the day. We hope everyone has a wonderful and relaxing weekend, and we look forward to seeing the children again next week for more learning and adventures together.
